About Us

Cards That Keep Coming, No Matter What

Forget 'Em Not was founded on a simple idea: physical cards carry a weight that digital messages never can. The effort and care taken into writing cards is a physical representation that says I was thinking of you.

We built Forget 'Em Not to make sure that message never stops reaching the people who matter most — even if you forget, even if you're traveling, and even if you're no longer here to send it yourself.

This is a completely new kind of service. We aren't just a card company — we're peace of mind, wrapped in an envelope.

Our Mission

"To ensure that everyone is remembered by their loved ones."

A One-of-a-Kind Service

No one else does this. We offer a unique service that understands nothing can replace the sentimental weight of a physical, handwritten card arriving in the mail.

Built for Every Stage of Life

Ideal for grandparents and older adults, but truly ageless. Doing long distance? Traveling abroad for years? Forget 'Em Not keeps the cards going no matter what.

Priceless Sentimental Value

Growing With You

The Process

How Forget 'Em Not Works

1

Place Your Order

Choose how many cards you need (5–15) and fill out your order form with recipient details and desired send dates.

2

Receive Your Box

We ship a box of blank cards and envelopes straight to your door. Fill them out in the order you want them sent.

3

Send Them Back

Mail the completed, labeled cards back to us. We store them safely until each date arrives.

4

We Mail Them Yearly

On the exact date you specified, every year, we send the card to your recipient — no reminders needed.
